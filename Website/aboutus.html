About Mind Anchor

Empowering minds through simple, effective memory games

Meet the Founder



Hello! I'm Augusto Zheng, a 15-year-old 10th grader from Argentina.
As the sole founder and creator of Mind Anchor,
I'm passionate about making a positive impact on the lives of
those struggling with memory loss.



My journey began with a simple idea:
to develop easy-to-use yet powerful memory games that could help people with dementia.
This vision has grown into Mind Anchor,
a platform dedicated to improving cognitive abilities through engaging and accessible games.

Our Mission

Empowering individuals with cognitive challenges through innovative memory games.
Creating accessible tools for maintaining and improving cognitive abilities.
Fostering a supportive community for those affected by memory loss.
Continuously evolving our platform to meet the unique needs of our users.
